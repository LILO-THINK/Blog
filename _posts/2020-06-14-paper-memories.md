In June I and Mari Nagem did a proposal for mapping the Goethe-Schiller-Archiv. And we decided to use paper like the subject and inspiration. We did a model of the building in the laser cutter for trying some ideas. And we delve deeply into the possibilities with paper... Ah, the project wasn't selected... But for me was the happiest prototype of 2020 with the best partner!
